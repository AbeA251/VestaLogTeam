Core Mission

Validate the VestaLog platform naturally supports how care is delivered in the real world. You will guide product enhancements from initial user feedback through to successful rollout, acting as the primary advocate for the daily caregiver experience.

Execution & Delivery

  • Workflow Translation: Convert core caregiving processes (shift handoffs, medication logging, incident reports) into clear, buildable product requirements.
  • Insights Management: Curate and organize a steady stream of user feedback, validating that field observations directly inform our product roadmap.
  • Rollout & Adoption: Partner with the team to guide feature launches, helping caregivers understand and adopt new workflows with minimal friction.

Impact Metrics

  • 01 Reduction in necessary clicks and time spent on core caregiver tasks.
  • 02 Feature adoption rate within the first 30 days of a new rollout.
  • 03 Clarity and readiness of product briefs handed over to engineering.
  • 04 Volume of actionable user feedback effectively integrated into product updates.
